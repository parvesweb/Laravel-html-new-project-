Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with me

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Most SEO campaigns show initial results within 3-6 months, while social media and PPC campaigns can deliver results within the first 30 days. During our consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline for your specific project.

What's your pricing structure?

I offer flexible pricing options including monthly retainers, project-based pricing, and hourly consulting. Monthly packages start at $499 for basic services, with custom enterprise solutions available. The final cost depends on your specific needs and goals.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. While I'm based in the US, I have experience working with businesses across North America, Europe, and Asia. Time zone differences are never a problem - I'm flexible with scheduling to accommodate international clients.

What industries do you specialize in?

I have extensive experience across multiple industries including e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, professional services, and B2B technology. My strategies are tailored to each industry's unique challenges and opportunities.

How do you measure success?

Success is measured through key performance indicators (KPIs) that we establish together at the beginning of our partnership. This typically includes metrics like website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, ROI, and other business-specific goals.

What's your communication process?

I maintain regular communication through weekly progress reports, monthly strategy calls, and 24/7 access via email and Slack. You'll always know exactly what's happening with your campaigns and can reach me whenever you have questions.
